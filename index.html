Instructions

You are a famous paranormal activist who has been invited to a spooky house to get rid of all ghosts that are living there. Tap (and drag) the screen to move the light cone of your flashlight around. Uncover all the hidden ghosts in the given timeframe. Wait until the ghost completely disappears or tap on that ghosts, but wait... you lose a few seconds if you do so.
